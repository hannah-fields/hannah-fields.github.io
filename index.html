About Me

I am currently a senior at the University of Maryland, majoring in multiplatform journalism with a minor in history. I am the Co-Editor in Chief of Unwind Magazine, a lifestyle and entertainment magazine on campus, and I copy edit for Mitzpeh, the university’s Jewish newspaper. In my time at Maryland, I have written for The Diamondback, The Left Bench, WMUC Sport and Unwind Magazine. In the summer after my freshman year, I was an editorial intern for the Central Pennsylvania Business Journal in Harrisburg, Pa. I worked for Andrea Koppel’s career-centered podcast, Time4Coffee, the following summer. This fall, I am interning at Baltimore Weddings, where I am writing, editing and assisting with Baltimore Magazine assignments. In my free time, I love to watch sports and stay active, and traveling is a big passion of mine. I am from PA but am hoping to stay in the D.C. area after finishing school.
